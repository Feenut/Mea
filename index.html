Our Story

Two years ago, I launched FD Bespoke to make a better way for funeral directors to get suits. But, most men in funeral service have everything they need. It's easy enough for them to pick up a suit at a department store and it fits well enough. There are tried and true brands that make a quality suit that can take a beating. Women don't have it so easy, and most have to settle for an inferior product when buying a suit. Most women's suit sizes just don't work and women's suits are built for the runway, not the prep room. In the next 10-20 years, women will take over the funeral industry, so this has to change. Mea exists to create a suit that female funeral directors love to wear because it can rise to the occasion of funeral service and make you look your best. My goal is to give women a suit that the men will be jealous of.

Brandon

What Makes Mea Different

We walk you through how to provide specific measurements so your suit fits exactly how you want.
We include real pockets inside and outside your suit jacket + on pants/skirts, so you don't have to carry a bag on the go.
Our signature stretch fabric is 8% spandex for high flexibility, 30% wool for breathability, and the rest made from synthetic fibers for durability. You'll love the way this suit feels.
We add an elastic adjuster in the waistband so your pants fit any time of the month and only use butterfly linings to give you more flexibility in the upper back.
You can customize just about anything on your suit: embroider your name on the inside, choose your lining, pocket locations, stitching, buttons, and more!
